HOW TO PLAY

  • Press the PLAY Button to start the game.
  • Press the colour which flashes on the screen.
  • The next colour will flash subsequently,then press the first color and the successive one.
  • The pattern of colours flashing is to be remembered by the player.
  • The player needs to press the colours in the same pattern to go to successive levels.
  • When pattern matching fails,the game is over.
