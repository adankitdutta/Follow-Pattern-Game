HOW TO PLAY

  • Click the PLAY Button to start the game.
  • Press the color which flashes on the screen.
  • Then next colour will flash,you need to press the first color and then the successive one.
  • The pattern of colour flashing must be remembered by the player.
  • Then the player needs to press the colours in that order to go to successive levels.
  • When pattern matching fails,the game is over.
